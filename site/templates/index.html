CCoWMU BootCTF

Welcome to BootCTF, the internal CTF playground of the Computer Club of Western Michigan University.

Who is your CTF and what does he do?

A Capture the Flag (CTF) is a computer security competition which consists of a series of challenges designed to emulate security vulnerabilities found in real world applications.

Contestants may be challenged in a wide array of computer security related areas, such as Reverse-engineering, network sniffing, protocol analysis, system administration, programming, and cryptanalysis.
